Animal House location: B Floor between B33 & B34

Laura Leebody is the Animal House technician

Basic Information

The Bangor Animal House is equipped with a range of animals designed to best suit your learning needs.

2 Mammals: Guinea Pigs, and mice.

5 Reptiles: Leopard Gecko, Crested Gecko, Corn Snake, Bearded Dragon and Tortoise.

3 Amphibians: African Clawed frogs, an Axolotl and a Tiger Salamander.

3 Invertebrates: Curly hair tarantula, Whip scorpion and Giant African Land Snails.

There is also a range of feeder insects.

Work Placement Hours

You can gain work placement hours by volunteering with Laura, but only if you are needed. It is your responsibility to sign in & out and get those hours signed by Laura. Work placement in Bangor Animal House, works on a 3 strike rule, sickness and lateness 3 times will see you removed from placement regardless of medical exemption.

Animal House Rules

You will be given a copy of the Animal House Rules.

You will be asked to read these and acknowledge that you have read and understood them by way of a sign off sheet.

No student without tetanus evidence will be allowed to work in the animal house.

If you are seen to be disregarding the rules, causing an issue to yourself, others or animals, you will be asked to leave the Animal House immediately.

Animal Requests

Animals in our Animal House are all our responsibilities and they need to go home at holidays, please see holiday request sheet on the Animal house Moodle page to add your interest.

If more than one request comes in for 1 animal, then the name of the student will be drawn from a hat.

Practicals and working in the animal house.

As per Animal House Rules, health and safety must be followed at all times when doing any thing in the animal house.

Lab coats and gloves, should be worn at all times!
